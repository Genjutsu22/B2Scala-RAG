# Needham-Schroeder Protocol (simplified)

## Agent: Alice

- **Behavior**:
  - Alice initiates a session with Bob.
  - She sends a message containing her nonce and identity, encrypted with Bob's public key.
  - She waits for a message from Bob, which should contain her nonce and his nonce, encrypted with her public key.
  - She sends back a message with Bob's nonce, encrypted with his public key.
